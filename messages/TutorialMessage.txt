Move the character with the W, A, S, D keys.
Shoot in the direction wanted with the arrow keys.
Kill mobs and go on loot boxes to get coins, and then buy items in shops!
For shops, press E or Escape to exit, and Enter to buy.
Press all the buttons on the current floor to go to the next!

Don't die; there's neither saves nor spawn points to set.
